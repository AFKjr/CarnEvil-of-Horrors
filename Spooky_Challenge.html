Escape the CarnEvil of Horrors

Where every choice leads to terror, and escape is never guaranteed.

The carnival appeared overnight, its twisted rides and flickering lights casting eerie shadows across the abandoned fairgrounds. No one remembers when it first arrived, but everyone knows the rules: once you enter, the only way out is to survive until dawn.

Every path you choose determines your fate. Will you escape through the Hall of Mirrors? Risk the Tunnel of Terror? Or brave the mysterious Big Top where laughter turns to screams? Your decisions matter here, and the carnival never forgets.

⚠ Warning ⚠

This carnival is not responsible for nightmares, disappearances, or permanent psychological changes. Enter at your own risk. Children must be accompanied by someone braver than themselves.

Choose Your Terror

Five attractions of pure horror await. Each choice determines your fate.

The Funhouse of Fear

Distorted mirrors show reflections that move on their own. Tilting floors lead you deeper into madness. The laughter you hear isn't recorded—it's coming from something inside.

Madame Zala's Fortune

The fortune teller knows your future, but her predictions come with a terrible price. Her crystal ball shows things you wish you could unsee. Will you ask about your fate?

The Carousel of Screams

Painted horses with eyes that follow you. Once the music starts, you can't get off. Round and round, faster and faster, while the world blurs into nightmare.

Tunnel of No Return

A winding dark passage where whispers echo off wet walls. Strange creatures lurk in the shadows. Multiple exits exist, but only one leads to freedom. Choose wisely.

The Big Top Nightmare

Clowns perform impossible acts under the spotless tent. Their smiles are too wide, their tricks too real. The audience never leaves, and soon you'll understand why.
